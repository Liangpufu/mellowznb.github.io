Privacy Policy
Last updated: January 2026

1. Overview

This application (“the App”) is a personal or limited-use application that integrates with the WHOOP API to retrieve health and fitness data for the purpose of generating personalized insights using AI models.

We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your data.

2. Data We Collect

With your explicit consent via WHOOP’s OAuth authorization, the App may access the following data from your WHOOP account:

Recovery data (e.g., recovery score, HRV, resting heart rate)

Sleep data (duration, efficiency, sleep stages)

Workout and strain data

Physiological metrics provided by WHOOP

No data is collected without your authorization.

3. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used only to:

Analyze health and fitness trends

Generate personalized summaries and insights

Provide training or recovery recommendations

Your data may be processed by third-party AI services (such as OpenAI) solely for generating insights and not for advertising or profiling.

4. Data Storage

Access tokens and data may be stored securely to enable app functionality.

Reasonable technical measures are taken to protect stored data.

Data is not sold, rented, or shared with unauthorized third parties.

5. Data Sharing

Your data is shared only with:

WHOOP (as part of authorized API access)

AI service providers strictly for data analysis purposes

We do not sell or monetize your personal data.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the app’s functionality or until you revoke access.

You may revoke WHOOP access at any time through your WHOOP account settings.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

Revoke access to your WHOOP data

Request deletion of stored data

Stop using the app at any time

8. Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data. However, no system can guarantee 100% security.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
